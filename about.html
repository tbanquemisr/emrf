About

Hi, I'm the human behind this blog.

I'm endlessly curious about how people learn, create, and build systems that support meaningful work. This corner of the internet is where I share what I'm experimenting with and the lessons that stick.

A quick snapshot

  • Work: Product-minded technologist building thoughtful digital experiences.
  • Curiosity: How creativity intersects with systems, habits, and tools.
  • Currently learning: Long-form storytelling and visual design fundamentals.
  • Beyond the screen: Analog journaling, trail running, and cooking for friends.

Why I write

Writing is how I think in public. It keeps me accountable to the questions I'm asking and the experiments I'm running. Publishing here invites conversation with people who are on a similar path or who see things differently enough to challenge my assumptions.

If any piece sparks a thought, I'd love to hear from you. Hit reply on the newsletter or say hello at hello@example.com.
